From Chandigarh to Global Impact A Journey of Clarity, Code, and Purpose

Early Life
Foundations

Born on March 18, 1976, in New Delhi, India, Sunil Dhawan grew up in a family that valued discipline, learning, and service. His father, an officer in the Indian Air Force, instilled in him a sense of structure and integrity that would shape his life and career.

Sunil completed his schooling in Chandigarh, where his curiosity for technology and problem-solving began to take root.

Early Career Learning from the Best

Sunil started his professional journey in Singapore, working with large consulting firms and global teams. This international exposure gave him a front-row seat to how top-tier systems were built and scaled.

He later moved to the United States, where he led software delivery for major retailers, gaining deep insights into enterprise technology and customer-centric innovation.

Entrepreneurial Leap The Birth of Abacasys

Global Expansion & Product Innovation

Between 2011 and 2025, Sunil guided Abacasys through a series of major milestones

2015 Acquired early customers in South Africa

2016 Launched Abaca ERP in India

2020 Introduced Abaca AIM, an AI-powered marketing automation & CRM tool

Expanded into the Middle East with operations in Dubai, Saudi Arabia, and Bahrain

Further growth into Europe, supporting a wide range of industries and clients

The Mission Today
Launching sunildhawan.com

Whether you're building something new or upgrading what already exists, my programs are designed to help you move forward with clarity, speed, and the right strategy.

His mission is clear:

To provide clarity, strategy, and execution for those looking to build, launch, or modernize their software—faster, smarter, and with confidence.

A Personal Note from Me

I've spent my life building teams, systems, products, and partnerships. I've seen the confusion founders face, the hesitation of business leaders, and the pressure of making the right tech decisions.

This platform was born from everything I've learned not just to showcase what I've done, but to help you do it better.

If you have an idea, a need, or even just a question... let's talk. You don't have to figure it all out on your own.

Clarity is the difference between an expensive mistake and a successful launch. My mission is to help you find that clarity before you build.
